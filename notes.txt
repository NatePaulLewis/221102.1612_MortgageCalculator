Mortgage Calculators
- Make a calculator that will tell the monthly payment on a mortgage
- The calculator should take inputs from the user
- Along with providing the monthly payment figure out how to show the interest paid
- Determines the financial implications of changes in one or more variables in a mortgage financing arrangement.
- Something that is not mentioned in Wikipedia calculation is the down payment. Implement this into the formula so that user can either input a percentage or dollar amount.
- Make sure to convert the figures into dollar amount and two figures after the decimal point.
- The major variables in a mortgage calculation include:
	- Loan Principal
	- Balance
	- Periodic Compound Interest Rate
	- Number of Payments Per Year
	- Total Number of Payments 
	- Regular Payment Amount
	- Other Cost Associated:
		- Local and State Tax
		- Insurance
